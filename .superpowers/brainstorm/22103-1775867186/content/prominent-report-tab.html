My Recommendation: Center Tab (Option B)

Why center tab over FAB:

  • Part of navigation - Users expect tabs at bottom, not floating buttons
  • Always same position - FAB can be obscured by content/map pins
  • Easier to tap - Larger target area, don't need to reach around content
  • Clearer purpose - Users understand "this is a navigation tab"
  • No content obstruction - FABs often cover map pins or content

Key Design Details

  • 30% larger than other tabs (flex-1.3 vs flex-1)
  • Red gradient (#DC2626 → #EF4444) for depth
  • White border (3px) creates separation from nav
  • Elevated shadow (0 4px 16px) creates floating effect
  • Heavier icon stroke (2.5 vs 2.0) matches urgency
  • Uppercase text with letter-spacing for emphasis
  • Negative margin (-8px top) makes it rise above nav
