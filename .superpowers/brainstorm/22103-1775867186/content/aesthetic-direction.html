Citizen App: Aesthetic Direction

What tone should this disaster reporting app have?

🚨
EMERGENCY
BOLD • URGENT • CLEAR

Urgent & Industrial

High-contrast emergency aesthetic. Bold reds, stark blacks, industrial yellow accents. Like emergency equipment - impossible to ignore.

BEST FOR: Stressful situations, quick decisions

🛡️
PROTECTION
CALM • CLEAR • SAFE

Calm & Reassuring

Soft blues, rounded corners, gentle animations. Feels like a safety app, not an alarm. Reduces panic during disasters.

BEST FOR: Community trust, regular use

🇵🇭
BANTAYOG
LOCAL • WARM • FILIPINO

Filipino Warmth

Sun-kissed yellows and warm earth tones. Bayanihan spirit - community helping community. Feels local and trusted.

BEST FOR: Cultural connection, accessibility
