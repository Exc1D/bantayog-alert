Design Principles

  • Blue foundation - Trust, reliability, calm during normal use
  • Red accents - Emergency button, alerts, high severity, critical CTAs
  • Inter/System-ui fonts - Clean, readable, available on all devices
  • Lucide icons - Consistent stroke weight (2.0 normal, 2.5 urgent)
  • Generous spacing - 44px min touch targets, 16-20px component padding
