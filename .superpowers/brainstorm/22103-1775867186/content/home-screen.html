My Recommendation: Map as Home

Why:

  • Spatial = survival - "Is the flood near MY house?" is the first question
  • Immediate context - See what's happening around you at a glance
  • Fewer taps - Tap pin → see details (vs scroll entire feed)
  • Emotional calmer - Visual overview is less overwhelming than scrolling text
  • Scales better - 100 pins on map is fine, 100 cards in feed is overwhelming

Feed becomes secondary - for "what happened recently?" or when you want to read details.

Or: Context-Aware Home?

Alternative: Remember user's last tab. If they last looked at Feed, open Feed. If last looked at Map, open Map. New users default to Map.

Trade-off:
Predictable (always map) vs personalized (remember last). For emergency apps, predictability usually wins.
